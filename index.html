24th October, 2024 — The Beginning 💖
Dear Ananya,

I'm not sure if you fully realize this, but you came into my life and changed everything — quietly, softly, and completely.

I remember when we first started talking. I didn't know then that I was meeting someone who would become family. But that's exactly what happened. Somewhere between random conversations, long calls, and moments of silence where we didn't even need words — you became my best friend. My little sister. My constant.

There were days when nothing seemed to make sense, when everything felt heavy and hard. You were there — not always with answers, but always with presence. That is a rare, rare thing. And I never want to forget to be grateful for it.

You carry so much inside that beautiful heart of yours. You're emotional in the most human way — you feel things deeply, love fiercely, and still choose to show up with a smile. That takes strength. More than you know.

I've seen how energetic and funny you can be — filling every room with laughter. And I've seen how quietly you hold pain. Both sides of you are real. Both sides deserve to be loved. And I do.

You are from Uttarakhand — a place of mountains and rivers and golden sunsets. I like to think you carry all of that within you: the groundedness of the mountains, the brightness of sunflowers, and the warmth of a home-cooked meal.

There were tough times. We both know that. But those didn't break anything — they built something. Trust. Real trust. The kind where you know someone will show up even when it's inconvenient. You were that person for me. I hope I was that for you too.

We are not just friends. We are chosen family. And I don't take that lightly.

I want you to know — on this Best Friends Day and every day after — that you are seen. You are appreciated. You are loved. Not because of what you do, but because of who you are.

I am so incredibly grateful that the universe somehow made our paths cross. I would choose you as my best friend in every version of this life.

Happy Best Friends Day, Bestie Sister. 🌻

May your mountains always be majestic, your sunflowers always be golden, and your heart always feel full.

Forever your best friend,
Always in your corner 💖
— Your Bestie Brother 🫶
